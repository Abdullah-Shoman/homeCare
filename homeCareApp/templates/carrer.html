Home Nursing Care

Job Description:

Job Summary: The home nurse/caregiver provides compassionate and skilled nursing care to individuals in their homes. They assist clients with activities of daily living, medication management, wound care, and other healthcare needs as prescribed by healthcare professionals. The home nurse/caregiver ensures the comfort, safety, and well-being of clients while fostering independence and promoting health.

Responsibilities:

  • Assessment and Monitoring: Conduct initial and ongoing assessments of clients' health status. Monitor vital signs, symptoms, and changes in condition. Report any concerns to appropriate healthcare professionals.
  • Personal Care: Assist clients with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and other personal hygiene tasks as needed.
  • Medication Management: Administer medications as prescribed by healthcare providers. Monitor medication schedules, refill prescriptions, and document administration accurately.
  • Nutrition and Hydration: Prepare and serve nutritious meals and snacks according to dietary requirements. Encourage adequate fluid intake and monitor nutritional status.

Qualifications:

  • Valid nursing license (RN or LPN) in the state of practice.
  • CPR certification.
  • Strong clinical skills, including assessment, medication administration, and wound care.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Experiances:

  • Bachelor Degree in Nursing is required.
  • Clinical Experience
  • Holds a license to practice the profession from the Nursing union and from the Palestinian Ministry of Health.
